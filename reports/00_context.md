## Revision Demystifies Small Language Models

### Core vocabulary

- **Reset**: train a smaller model from scratch.
- **Revision**: prune a larger pretrained model, retrain, and evaluate.
- **Residue**: capability or structure that survives compression.

### Observation

Small language models can be built by reset or revision.

### Engineering statement

Smaller language models are not merely fewer parameters.
They are the result of decisions about what to preserve,
what to revise, and how to measure success.
